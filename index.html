Began using in August, 2021. Migrated from Artix, identical to Arch GNU/Linux.

Variant: exherbo-x86_64-pc-linux-musl

Rationale for proceeding with Exherbo

  • The people, such as Hiecko Becker, are amiable people.
  • No insurmountable issues. For issues such as installation issues, the resolution is not overly complex.
  • Participation in decentralised package maintanence; will complete some of these projects once programming knowledge accumulates.
  • Allows for setting of LibreSSL instead of OpenSSL though this action appears less promising as time passes.
  • Enhances rewarding, DIY attitude.
